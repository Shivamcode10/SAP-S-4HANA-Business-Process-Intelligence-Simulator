# Order-to-Cash Simulation

## Scenario

1. Sales Order Created

   - Material: Laptop  
   - Quantity: 2  

2. Delivery  
   - Product dispatched  
   - Stock reduced  

3. Billing

4. Payment  
   - Customer pays  
   - AR cleared
